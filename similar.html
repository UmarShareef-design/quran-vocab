💡 How to Tell Them Apart

Listen for the vowel: Many pairs differ only by a single vowel (e.g., Qalb vs Kalb). Pay attention to the short a, i, or u sounds — they completely change the meaning.

Watch the diacritics: In voweled Quranic text, the harakat (fatḥah, ḍammah, kasrah) make the difference obvious. Learn to read with vowel marks and the confusion disappears.

Use context: When you hear "Salaah" (Prayer) vs "Salaam" (Peace) in a verse, the surrounding words always make the intended meaning clear.
